Reported on Saturday 17th September, 2022.

Mark Fullbrook is a political strategist and lobbyist, who has served under Margaret Thatcher, John Major, and Liz Truss as Deputy Head of the Conservative Party, Head of Strategy, and Downing Street Chief of Staff. The Sante Group, to which he was adviser, was awarded a £680 million PPE grant during the Covid-19 pandemic. As Liz Truss's most senior adviser, he has a hand in shaping government policy. He has been interviewed by the FBI over his relationship with a banker who gave the governor of Puerto Rico $300,000 for her re-election campaign. The donation was allegedly made on the condition that she sacked the head of the Island's financial regulator. Several people have already been charged or convicted.
